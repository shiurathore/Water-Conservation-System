Smart Water Saving Guideline

Discover practical ways to reduce daily water consumption and make a sustainable impact.

Fix Leaks

Repair dripping taps and pipes immediately to save liters of water daily.

Rainwater Harvesting

Collect rainwater in tanks for gardening, cleaning, and household use.

Water Plants Wisely

Water plants early in the morning or late evening to reduce evaporation.

Spread Awareness

Educate your family and community about the importance of water conservation.

Recycle Water

Reuse kitchen water for cleaning or gardening wherever possible.

Short Showers

Reduce shower time and use a bucket instead, cutting down wastage.

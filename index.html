GUIDELINE

Water Saving Guideline

Household

  • Fix leaking taps quickly.
  • Turn off tap while brushing teeth.
  • Use a bucket instead of shower.
  • Collect rainwater for plants.
  • Use washing machine with full load.

Agriculture

  • Use drip irrigation instead of flooding.
  • Water crops in morning or evening.
  • Grow crops that need less water.
  • Collect rainwater in ponds.
  • Check soil moisture before watering.

Industry

  • Recycle and reuse factory water.
  • Fix pipe leaks.
  • Use water-saving machines.
  • Treat water before release.
  • Use efficient cooling systems.
